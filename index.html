What we do

Cadmus Language Solutions focuses on one thing: helping providers communicate clearly with patients who have limited English proficiency, without adding chaos to your day.

  • On-demand remote interpreting (phone or video)
  • Scheduled sessions for longer visits
  • Culturally aware interpreters with medical vocabulary

Why clinics choose Cadmus

Designed with input from real clinics so it fits into your workflows instead of fighting them.

  • Responsive by design — aiming for under 10 minutes to connect
  • Human-first, not call-center first
  • Flexible & local
  • Compliance-minded

Who we work with

How the early-access pilot works

We’re starting with a focused 30-day pilot to learn with a small group of clinics and refine Cadmus around real-world use.

  • 1. Quick intro call — 15–20 minutes
  • 2. Simple setup — secure number or request link
  • 3. 30-day pilot — track response times & feedback
  • 4. Review — decide next steps
